About

Hi, I am Shrikanth a newly minted Ph.D. from North Carolina State University. I worked with Dr.Menzies in his super cool Real-world Artificial Intelligence for Software Engineering lab. Mostly, I enjoy examining software engineering data for a living. Previously, I was a Technology R&D Specialist at Accenture Labs (Software Engineering Group), exploring crowdsourcing and log analytics issues. Before that, as a software engineer, I managed the software development life-cycle of products built for electrical and banking domains at ABB India Ltd and Infosys Ltd, respectively. Currently, I am writing my doctoral dissertation titled "Taming Confusions in Software Engineering."
